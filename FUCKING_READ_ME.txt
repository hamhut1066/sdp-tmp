To run:
Connect the robot to the computer
open motor_head/motor_head.ino
Click on the arrow button to verify(compile) the code
Unplug the battery
Click on the button next to it to upload it to the arduino.
Click on the button that looks like a magnifying glass 
In the textbox enter a number on the number pad.


Its fairly basic to operate  - 8 will go forward, 2 to go backward...
